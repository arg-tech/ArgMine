Nospeaker: Presentation and First Reading (Standing Order No. 57)Pete Wishart, supported by Deidre Brock, Tommy Sheppard, Gavin Newlands and Patrick Grady, presented a Bill to make provision for the House of Commons to nominate the Prime Minister and approve appointments to the Cabinet; to establish the office of Acting Prime Minister; and for connected purposes.Bill read the First time; to be read a Second time tomorrow, and to be printed (Bill 420).
