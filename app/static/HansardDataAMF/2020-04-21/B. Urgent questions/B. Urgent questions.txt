Nospeaker: 1) In respect of any day to which order (A. Scrutiny proceedings) applies, a Member may apply to the Speaker for leave to ask an urgent question under this order.2) An urgent question is one which, in the Speaker’s opinion, is of an urgent character and relates to a matter of public importance.
